Privacy Policy

At Tanzania E-Service Portal, we are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with Tanzania’s Data Protection Act. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Data Collection

We collect your email, hashed password, and TOTP secret (encrypted) during registration. Google Sign-In users provide their email and Google ID.

Data Use

Your data is used to authenticate your identity, enable 2FA, and provide access to e-services. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We use HTTPS, AES encryption, bcrypt hashing, and secure session management to protect your data. QR codes are deleted after use.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact support for assistance.

For questions, contact us at support@eservice.tz.
